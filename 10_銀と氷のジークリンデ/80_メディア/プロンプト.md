Airy J-pop ballad with delicate piano and soft pads, 
female solo vocals, dry and close-mic'd throughout verses, 
no echo on lead vocal, no doubling effect. 
Chorus blooms with wide reverb on instruments only, 
subtle strings, and light stacked harmonies as background only — 
lead vocal stays clean and upfront. 
Light percussion enters only in the chorus for lift, 
with a gentle rise into the final refrain and a tender, fading outro.
